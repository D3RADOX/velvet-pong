# 09. Production and QA Checklist

**Velvet Pong**

**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** June 2026

*This checklist ensures comprehensive coverage of all production, polish, and quality assurance items required for a premium AAA release. Items are organized by category and phase. Use as a living document; mark items complete with date and responsible party.*

---

## 1. Pre-Production & Design Completeness (Phase 0)

- [ ] All design documents reviewed and approved by creative director and stakeholders.
- [ ] Art direction bible, style guide, and tone document signed off.
- [ ] Full asset list with specifications and production priorities finalized.
- [ ] Technical architecture and performance targets documented and approved.
- [ ] Narrative bible, character bibles, and relationship system design complete.
- [ ] Gameplay mechanics, balance pillars, and accessibility guidelines documented.
- [ ] Risk assessment and mitigation plan approved.
- [ ] Budget, schedule, and team structure locked.
- [ ] Key art, logo, and marketing mood references approved.

---

## 2. Vertical Slice / Prototype (Phase 1)

- [ ] Core Pong physics and controls feel excellent in isolation.
- [ ] One fully detailed, lit, and optimized club environment playable.
- [ ] 3+ primary characters rigged, with idles, basic reactions, and at least 3 dance animations each.
- [ ] Basic environmental interaction and 2–3 power-up systems functional and visually spectacular.
- [ ] Simple affinity/relationship prototype with 1–2 full dialogue scenes.
- [ ] Dynamic lighting and music reactivity demonstrated.
- [ ] Performance targets met on target hardware (PC high/ultra, PS5, Series X).
- [ ] Internal playtest feedback collected and incorporated (minimum 10 testers).
- [ ] Vertical slice presentation prepared and delivered successfully.

---

## 3. Art & Animation Production

- [ ] All hero environment assets modeled, textured, and integrated with proper LODs and Nanite setup.
- [ ] All primary NPCs fully rigged, with complete animation libraries (locomotion, dance, dialogue, reactions, match-specific).
- [ ] Secondary characters and background assets at required fidelity.
- [ ] All paddle archetypes modeled, textured, and integrated with effects.
- [ ] Ball variants and power-up VFX complete and performant.
- [ ] Cloth, hair, and physics simulation tuned and stable across all characters and outfits.
- [ ] Facial animation and blendshapes complete for all speaking characters.
- [ ] All materials authored to PBR standards with multiple variants where specified.
- [ ] Optimization pass complete (draw calls, memory, streaming).
- [ ] All assets named, organized, and version-controlled per pipeline standards.

---

## 4. Gameplay Systems

- [ ] Full physics implementation (spin, curve, environmental bounces, dynamic friction).
- [ ] Complete power-up and event system with balancing and telegraphing.
- [ ] Club Energy meter and systemic feedback loop fully functional.
- [ ] AI opponents with distinct personalities and appropriate difficulty scaling.
- [ ] Customization system (paddles, tables, avatar) complete with mechanical and visual variants.
- [ ] Progression, experience, and unlock systems implemented and balanced.
- [ ] Club management layer (investments, staff decisions) functional with clear feedback.
- [ ] Accessibility options fully implemented and tested (controls, visuals, audio, subtitles).
- [ ] Practice mode, tutorials, and onboarding complete.

---

## 5. Narrative & Role-Playing Systems

- [ ] Full dialogue system with branching, affinity tracking, and relationship consequences.
- [ ] All story chapters playable end-to-end with multiple paths tested.
- [ ] All primary NPCs have complete voice acting, facial animation, and body language.
- [ ] Multiple endings implemented and accessible based on player choices.
- [ ] Side content and optional relationship scenes complete.
- [ ] Club management decisions have measurable narrative and gameplay impact.
- [ ] Photo mode and social sharing features functional.

---

## 6. Audio Production

- [ ] Full original soundtrack (20–30 tracks) with stems for dynamic mixing.
- [ ] All SFX recorded or synthesized, implemented, and mixed (500+ unique sounds).
- [ ] Full voice acting recorded, processed, and integrated for primary cast and key scenes.
- [ ] Ambient beds, spot effects, and crowd reactions complete.
- [ ] Dynamic music and audio reactivity to gameplay intensity and club energy.
- [ ] UI sounds, haptic feedback design, and accessibility audio options.
- [ ] Final mix and mastering pass for all platforms.

---

## 7. Multiplayer & Online

- [ ] Local split-screen and shared-screen multiplayer fully functional and balanced.
- [ ] Online lobby, matchmaking, and session management complete.
- [ ] Ranked and casual modes with proper skill-based matchmaking.
- [ ] Netcode robust under simulated latency and packet loss.
- [ ] Anti-cheat and fair play systems implemented and tested.
- [ ] Private lobbies and custom rules support.
- [ ] Leaderboards, replays, and social features operational.

---

## 8. Polish, Balance, and Optimization

- [ ] Comprehensive balancing pass across all modes, difficulties, and player skill levels.
- [ ] Visual polish sprint ("juice"): enhanced particles, screen effects, camera work, timing.
- [ ] Animation polish: transitions, blending, secondary motion, and reactivity.
- [ ] Performance optimization to ship targets (frame rate, load times, memory).
- [ ] Memory and streaming optimization complete.
- [ ] Bug database triaged; all critical and high-priority bugs resolved.
- [ ] Regression testing complete after all major changes.

---

## 9. Quality Assurance & Certification

- [ ] Full platform certification testing (Sony TRC, Microsoft XR, Steam Deck, etc.).
- [ ] Comprehensive QA coverage: functional, regression, stress, compatibility, localization.
- [ ] Accessibility audit and compliance verification.
- [ ] Localization (text, audio, UI) complete and tested for supported languages.
- [ ] Age rating submission (ESRB, PEGI, etc.) prepared and submitted.
- [ ] Security and privacy compliance verified.
- [ ] Final release candidate approved by all leads and stakeholders.

---

## 10. Marketing, Launch, and Post-Launch Readiness

- [ ] Key art, trailers, screenshots, and store assets finalized.
- [ ] Press kit, developer blog content, and community assets prepared.
- [ ] Day-one patch content identified and prepared.
- [ ] Post-launch support plan (DLC roadmap, seasons, balance updates) documented.
- [ ] Community management and moderation tools ready.
- [ ] Analytics and telemetry systems operational.
- [ ] Launch event and influencer outreach plan executed.

---

## 11. Ongoing & Living Checklist Items

- [ ] All documentation kept up to date throughout production.
- [ ] Regular playtests scheduled and feedback incorporated.
- [ ] Performance budgets monitored and enforced at every milestone.
- [ ] Tone and content review gates passed at each major narrative or visual delivery.
- [ ] Pipeline and tool improvements documented and shared with team.

---

**Usage Notes:**
- This checklist should be converted into a living tracker (Jira, Notion, or spreadsheet) with owners, due dates, and status.
- Items may be expanded with sub-checklists during production.
- All items must be verified as complete before advancing to the next phase or milestone.
- Final sign-off requires creative director, technical director, producer, and QA lead approval.

*End of Document 09*
