# 03. Narrative, Role-Playing, and Character Designs

**Velvet Pong**

**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** June 2026

---

## 1. Narrative Overview and Themes

Velvet Pong's story explores ambition, performance, belonging, and the cost of success within the glittering but competitive world of high-end nightlife. The player character arrives at The Velvet Lounge seeking opportunity and quickly discovers that the Pong table is both arena and social ladder.

**Central Themes:**
- The artistry and athleticism of performance (dance and competitive play).
- Building authentic relationships versus transactional alliances.
- The tension between personal glory and collective club success.
- Reinvention and self-definition in an environment that rewards image and substance.
- Respect, consent, and empowerment in adult entertainment spaces (handled maturely and positively).

Tone: Sophisticated, witty, occasionally dramatic or sensual, but always respectful. Humor arises from character personalities and situational irony rather than crude stereotypes.

## 2. Player Protagonist

**Name:** Customizable (default suggestions: "Alex Rivera," "Jordan Hale," etc.)

**Background Selection** (affects starting affinities and dialogue options):
- **The Ambitious Outsider**: Former competitive gamer or athlete. High starting skill, lower initial social connections.
- **The Charmer**: Experienced in service/hospitality. Strong dialogue and relationship bonuses.
- **The Performer**: Former dancer or stage talent. Natural affinity with dancers and showmanship bonuses.
- **The Strategist**: Business or management background. Better club management mechanics and long-term planning.

**Customization:** Deep MetaHuman-based appearance editor (face, body, hair, tattoos, clothing style). Voice selection (several options with pitch and style variations). Starting outfit and signature paddle.

**Arc:** From newcomer proving themselves at the table, to influential figure shaping the club's future, to potential legend or cautionary tale depending on choices.

## 3. Key Non-Player Characters (NPCs)

### Core Cast (8–10 Primary)

1. **Elena Voss** – The Star Headliner Dancer  
   Elegant, confident, perfectionist. Signature style: Fluid, powerful pole and floor work. Relationship path: Professional respect → deep artistic partnership → romantic tension (optional). Unlocks: Signature power-up "Voss Vortex" (strong spin manipulation), special co-op performance matches.

2. **Marcus "The Enforcer" Kane** – Head of Security / Bouncer  
   Stoic, loyal, protective. Former boxer. Plays Pong with raw power and intimidation. Relationship: Rivalry → mutual respect → trusted ally. Unlocks: Defensive paddle upgrades, "Bouncer's Wall" environmental modifier.

3. **Sofia "Sofie" Laurent** – Club Manager / Owner's Right Hand  
   Sharp, ambitious, pragmatic. Master of soft power and scheduling. Relationship: Transactional → genuine alliance → potential power struggle or partnership. Unlocks: Club management tools, exclusive venue upgrades.

4. **Darius "DJ D" Moreau** – Resident DJ and Music Curator  
   Charismatic, creative, connected. Controls the soundtrack and lighting cues. Relationship: Creative collaboration → friendship. Unlocks: Dynamic music-reactive power-ups, custom soundtracks for matches.

5. **Isabella "Bella" Ruiz** – Rising New Dancer / Protégé  
   Talented, eager, slightly naive. Represents the "new blood" the player can mentor or exploit. Relationship: Mentor/mentee → deep bond or betrayal path. Unlocks: Fresh power-up ideas, emotional story beats.

6. **Victor "Vic" Lang** – Rival Pong Champion from Competing Club  
   Arrogant, skilled, flashy. Primary antagonist/antagonist-turned-ally. Multiple encounters throughout campaign. Relationship: Heated rivalry → grudging respect → possible redemption or downfall arc.

7. **Regina "Reggie" Holt** – Veteran Bartender and Information Broker  
   Wise, sarcastic, knows everyone's secrets. Provides gossip, side quests, and relationship advice. Unlocks: Hidden information that affects dialogue and affinity gains.

8. **The Owner ("Mr. or Ms. Veil")** – Mysterious Club Proprietor  
   Elegant, enigmatic, rarely seen. Ultimate authority. Relationship: Professional distance → earned respect or confrontation. Major story branch point in later chapters.

### Supporting Cast

- Background dancers (4–6 recurring with distinct looks and personalities).
- Regular patrons (VIPs, regulars, hecklers) with short dialogue and reactions.
- Rival club staff and champions for tournament arcs.

All primary NPCs have:
- Full voice acting (high-quality, celebrity or strong character actors recommended).
- Rich facial animation and body language via MetaHuman or equivalent.
- Multiple outfit tiers that evolve with story/relationship progress (more elaborate or personalized costumes).
- Affinity system (0–100) with thresholds for new dialogue, scenes, gameplay bonuses, and endings.

## 4. Relationship and Affinity System

Affinity is gained through:
- Strong performance in matches while the NPC is present or featured.
- Dialogue choices that align with the NPC's values and personality.
- Completing personal side stories or favors.
- Consistent tipping and respectful behavior (mechanical representation of respect).

High affinity unlocks:
- Unique paddle visual/mechanical variants.
- Signature power-ups or environmental effects tied to that character.
- Private story scenes (some romantic, some platonic/professional — all consensual and respectful).
- Co-op or special exhibition matches.
- Influence over club decisions involving that character.

Low or negative affinity can lead to:
- Increased difficulty or sabotage in matches.
- Locked story branches.
- Public confrontations or club drama that affects Energy meter.

The system encourages genuine engagement rather than min-maxing; multiple viable paths exist to high affinity with different characters.

## 5. Story Structure and Branching

**Chapter Structure (5–7 Chapters + Epilogue):**

- **Chapter 1: Arrival** – Introduction to club, first matches, meeting core cast, establishing reputation.
- **Chapter 2: Rising Star** – First rival encounters, building initial alliances, discovering club tensions.
- **Chapter 3: Spotlight** – Major tournament or showcase event, deeper relationship development, first major choice point.
- **Chapter 4: Fractures** – Internal conflicts, betrayals, or external threats (rival club takeover attempt).
- **Chapter 5: Reckoning** – Climactic confrontations, high-stakes matches with narrative consequences.
- **Chapter 6: Legacy** (optional) – Post-climax rebuilding or new challenges.
- **Epilogue** – Multiple endings based on overall reputation, key relationships, and final choices (6–8 primary endings: triumphant legend, quiet influencer, dramatic exit, club savior, etc.).

Branching is meaningful but manageable: Major decisions gated behind affinity thresholds and key matches. Side content expands the world without requiring completion for main story.

---

## 6. Role-Playing Mechanics and Player Agency

- **Dialogue Trees**: Context-sensitive, with personality stats (Charm, Intimidation, Empathy, Wit) influencing available options and success chances.
- **Club Management Decisions**: Invest earnings in upgrades, resolve staff disputes, set performance schedules — these have gameplay (new power-ups, table features) and narrative consequences.
- **Match Stakes**: Certain story matches have added pressure (e.g., "If you lose, you lose influence or a relationship point").
- **Photo Mode Integration**: Capture and share "iconic moments" that can affect in-game reputation or unlock social features.

Player agency is high: Different play styles and relationship focuses lead to distinctly different playthroughs and endings while preserving the core satisfying Pong loop.
